Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us

What information should I include in my message?

To help us respond to your inquiry more efficiently, please include your full name, contact information, and details about your specific need or question. If you're a job seeker, mentioning your field of expertise and experience level is helpful. For employers, information about your hiring needs and company will allow us to provide more relevant assistance.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly at (1) 347-680-2869 during business hours.

Do I need an appointment to visit your office?

While walk-ins are welcome during our business hours, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that a consultant is available to meet with you. This also allows us to prepare for your visit and provide more personalized service.

Can I submit my resume through this contact form?

While you can mention your interest in submitting your resume through the contact form, we recommend creating an account on our website and uploading your resume through our secure job seeker portal. This ensures your information is properly entered into our database and can be matched with appropriate opportunities.
