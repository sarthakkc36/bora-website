Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about posting jobs

How long will my job posting be visible?

The visibility duration depends on the package you select. Basic package provides 30 days visibility, Premium offers 60 days, and Enterprise gives you 90 days of visibility.

How will I receive applications from candidates?

Candidates can apply through our platform, and you'll receive applications via email. You can also direct applicants to your own application system by providing an external URL.

Can I edit my job posting after it's published?

Yes, you can edit your job posting after it's published. Simply log in to your account, go to your dashboard, and select the job you want to edit. However, major changes may require review by our team.

Is there a limit to how many jobs I can post?

Each package has its own limit. The Basic and Premium packages include one job posting, while the Enterprise package allows you to post up to 5 jobs. If you need to post more jobs, please contact our sales team for custom packages.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise packages. All payments are processed securely.
