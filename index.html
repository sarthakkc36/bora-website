Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How does your job placement process work?

Our job placement process begins with an initial consultation to understand your skills, experience, and career goals. We then match you with suitable opportunities from our extensive job database. Once we find potential matches, we'll prepare you for interviews and provide support throughout the hiring process until you secure the right position.

Is there a fee for job seekers?

No, our job placement services are completely free for job seekers. Our fees are covered by the hiring companies that partner with us to find qualified candidates.

What types of positions do you fill?

We work across multiple industries including IT, healthcare, finance, education, marketing, and administrative roles. We place candidates in positions ranging from entry-level to executive roles, both permanent and contract-based.

How long does it typically take to find a job?

The timeline varies depending on factors such as your skills, experience, industry, and current market conditions. Some candidates are placed within days, while others may take a few weeks or months. We work diligently to find opportunities that match your qualifications as quickly as possible.

How can employers partner with B&H Employment?

Employers can contact us through our website or directly via phone or email. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your hiring needs, company culture, and recruitment challenges. From there, we'll develop a customized recruitment strategy and present you with pre-screened, qualified candidates that match your requirements.
