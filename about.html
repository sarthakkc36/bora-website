B&H Employment & Consultancy Inc is a premier job placement agency based in New York, dedicated to connecting talented professionals with leading employers across the United States.

We understand that finding the right job or the right candidate can be challenging in today's competitive market. That's why we've built our reputation on providing personalized, high-quality recruitment services that meet the unique needs of both job seekers and employers.

Our team of experienced recruiters brings industry-specific knowledge and a passion for matching the right talent with the right opportunity. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, integrity, and building long-lasting relationships with our clients and candidates.

Our Mission & Values

Guided by our core principles to deliver exceptional service

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve as a bridge between talented professionals and forward-thinking employers, creating meaningful employment opportunities while helping businesses grow with the right talent. We strive to transform the recruitment process into a seamless, personalized experience that values the unique needs of both candidates and clients.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every professional finds fulfilling work that matches their skills and aspirations, and where every organization has access to the talent they need to thrive. B&H Employment & Consultancy aims to be recognized as the most trusted and effective employment partner in the industries we serve, known for our expertise, integrity, and results.

Integrity

We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards, honesty, and transparency in all our interactions.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering exceptional service and continuously improving our processes and methodologies.

Relationships

We build long-lasting relationships based on trust, understanding, and mutual respect.

Results

We focus on delivering tangible outcomes for our clients and candidates through efficient and effective solutions.

Our Recruitment Process

A structured approach to finding the perfect match

1

Understanding Requirements

We start by thoroughly understanding the job requirements and company culture for employers, or career goals and preferences for job seekers.

2

Sourcing & Screening

Our team identifies qualified candidates through our extensive network, database, and targeted search strategies, followed by comprehensive screening.

3

Interview Preparation

We prepare candidates for interviews with employer-specific insights, while providing employers with detailed candidate profiles.

4

Selection & Onboarding

We facilitate the final selection process and support both parties through offer negotiation and the onboarding transition.

5

Follow-Up & Support

Our relationship continues with regular check-ins to ensure satisfaction and address any concerns from both employers and placed candidates.
